## About This Project

This is a Product Management capstone built for the **E-Cell IIT Guwahati × IndusInd Bank Product Matters (PM 6.0)** course.

**The brief:** As a Product Manager at IndusInd General Insurance, reimagine how customers discover, purchase, and manage general insurance — moving beyond a one-time purchase into an ongoing, proactive protection experience embedded within the IndusInd Bank ecosystem.

## The Problem

Insurance in India remains fragmented and reactive:
- Customers own multiple policies across different providers with no unified view
- Technical jargon (IDV, co-pay, NCB) makes coverage genuinely confusing
- Claims are slow and documentation-heavy — the single biggest driver of non-renewal
- Customers only engage with insurance after something has already gone wrong

## The Solution — IndusInd Protect

A unified insurance layer built directly into the existing IndusInd banking app, centered on three pillars:

| Pillar | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Discover & Decide** | A "Protection Score" and life-stage-aware recommendations, powered by data the bank already has — no lengthy intake forms |
| **Manage & Renew** | One dashboard for every policy, across every provider, with WhatsApp renewal nudges before lapse, not after |
| **Claim & Trust** | Self-serve claims with AI-assisted self-inspection, targeting under-48-hour resolution |

## What's in This Prototype

A 15-screen clickable walkthrough covering the full customer journey:

- **Discover & Decide** — Banking Home, Protection Score, Recommendations, Policy Detail
- **Manage & Renew** — Purchase Form, Confirmation, Unified Dashboard, Link a Policy, WhatsApp Renewal
- **Claim & Trust** — File a Claim, Self-Inspection Capture, Claim Status Tracker
- **Bonus** — AI Assistant, Personalization Nudge, Ecosystem Perks & Monetization
