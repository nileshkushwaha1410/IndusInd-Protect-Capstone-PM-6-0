# IndusInd Protect — Project Build Order

### E-Cell IIT Guwahati × IndusInd Bank | Product Matters (PM 6.0) Capstone

This document tracks the sequence in which the capstone was built, from problem understanding through final deployment prep.

---

## 1. Problem Understanding
Read and internalized the IndusInd Bank × E-Cell brief. Defined product scope as a **unified, multi-category insurance platform** (health, motor, home, travel) rather than a single-category solution, targeting IndusInd Bank's existing banking customers.

## 2. Product Strategy
- Industry & market overview — penetration data, regulatory shifts (IRDAI Bima Sugam, Use-and-File), embedded insurance trends
- Competitor analysis — ICICI Lombard (IL TakeCare), Digit, Acko, Policybazaar
- Target persona — primary "Practical Priya," secondary "First-Job Farhan"
- Problem statement & opportunity sizing — TAM/SAM/SOM
- Product vision & value proposition

## 3. Product Execution
- North Star Metric — Active Protected Customers (APC), 1 Lakh by Month 6 — plus supporting metric tree
- GTM strategy — three phases: warm-base activation → habit & trust building → ecosystem expansion
- Product roadmap — Q1 (Foundation) through Q4 (Scale & Intelligence)
- Assumptions & risks

## 4. Product Design
- User journey mapping — Priya's full lifecycle across Discover & Decide, Manage & Renew, Claim & Trust
- Key user flows — Discover & Buy, Link a Policy, Renew, Claim
- Feature prioritization — RICE framework (Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort)
- Wireframe/screen planning for the prototype

## 5. Clickable Prototype (Core Build)
Built 12 core screens as a single self-contained HTML/CSS/JS file (`index.html`), styled in IndusInd's maroon/gold brand identity, covering the four key flows end to end.

## 6. Bonus Section (Written)
Documented AI-powered experiences, claims automation, fraud detection concepts, personalization engine, monetization strategy, high-level technical architecture, and data/analytics instrumentation.

## 7. Presentation Deck
Compressed all prior sections (Strategy, Execution, Design) into a 15-slide `.pptx` deck, following IndusInd's brand palette and the brief's submission cap.

## 8. Bonus Section (Added to Prototype)
Extended the prototype from 12 → 15 screens by turning 3 Bonus concepts into actual clickable screens:
- AI Assistant (plain-language insurance Q&A)
- Personalization Nudge (life-event-triggered offer with disclosure)
- Ecosystem Perks & Monetization (bundled perks, Priority-tier upsell)

Then updated the deck's prototype slide to match the new 15-screen count.

## 9. LinkedIn Post
Drafted two post variants (problem → solution → key learnings), tagging E-Cell IIT Guwahati with relevant hashtags, plus visual options pulled from the deck.

## 10. Demo Video
Captured all 15 prototype screens via automated screenshots, composited into a branded ~37-second walkthrough video with section-grouped captions and intro/outro cards.

## 11. Deployment Prep
- Finalized GitHub repository naming (`Indusind-Protect-Capstone-PM-6-0`)
- Wrote repository README and description
- Documented GitHub Pages deployment steps
- Mapped every brief requirement to its final submission asset (deck / prototype link / LinkedIn post link)

## 12. Final Consistency Audit
Verified deck validity and slide count, confirmed prototype-to-deck screen parity, checked all in-app navigation links resolve correctly, and validated the demo video and supporting documents before submission.

---

*This order broadly mirrors the brief's own Deliverables structure — Strategy → Execution → Design → Prototype → Bonus — with a final sync pass once new Bonus screens were added to the prototype after the deck was first built.*
